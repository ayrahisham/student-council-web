Meetings Are Powerful Weapon!
Knowing that school is competitive in nature, students need regular personal interaction more than ever. Although telephone and online communication allow for a convenient and quick means of interaction with one another and provide an avenue for quick sharing of ideas, scheduling members for a meeting still creates a different effect on the council. Negotiations are actually best dealt with over meetings.Through meetings, the council can create bonds with one another.

NEWS
Meetings Help A Team Arrive At A Consensus
One reason why a meeting is important is because it helps a team to reach a common decision when urgent and crucial matters need to be discussed and brain stormed through personal interaction. Email or texts may not effectively iron out any issues arising in the school. Notice that in situations like this, students are actively interacting and exchanging opinions. Through a meeting, members are able to compare plans and propositions before students take on conflicting directions.

ELECTIONS
Meetings Keep the Council on Track!
Regular sessions do not just strengthen bonds and relationships among members, but it also keep teams on track regarding school matters. For example, when a team is given a huge project, a kick-off meeting is the best way to delegate tasks and assignments. In order to keep track of the work progress, regular meetings can be conducted where team members share each other’s development. The whole team is constantly updated with how well the project goes and what things need to be worked on.

PROFILE
Why Meetings Are Essential?
Conducting a meeting makes it easy for students to deliver any important updates at one time and at the same time to the members. Meetings also provide an opportunity for the council to acknowledge the accomplishments of group members through recognition. Although there are petty arguments and disagreements of each other’s ideas, the committee is there to control the situation. The important thing is that everyone arrives at a consensus by the end of the session. With uniformity in decision making, the goals are achieved in no time. From the broad discussion, commitment to a decision and course of action are guaranteed from the members.
