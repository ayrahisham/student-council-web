Why Team Building Is Essential?"
At the end of the day, team building in the workplace enables better communication, better relationships and ultimately increases productivity.
- Vice-Treasurer, UOW Student Council 2017

HOME
Facilitates Better Communication
Activities that create discussion enable open communication among members, and between members and committee. This can improve school relationships and in turn, the quality of work done.

NEWS
Motivates Student Council Members
Team leadership and team building go hand in hand. The more comfortable members are to express their ideas and opinions, the more confident they will become. This will motivate them to take on new challenges.

ELECTIONS
Promotes Creativity
Taking a team outside of a school setting and exposing them to new experiences will force them to think outside of their normal routine. Working together with other team members can ignite creativity and fresh ideas, which are great qualities to bring back to school.

PROFILE
Developing Problem Solving Skills
A crisis can happen at any time. Team building activities that require coworkers to work together to solve problems can improve the ability to think rationally and strategically. Teams that are able to determine when a problem arises and know what they can do about it, can then effectively take charge when a real crisis occurs.

EVENTS
Breaks the Barrier
Team building increases the trust factor with members. Often in academic settings there is a disconnect between the leadership team and members because the members sense too large of a gap between the two. Team building exercises give leadership the opportunity, which can do wonders for individual morale.
