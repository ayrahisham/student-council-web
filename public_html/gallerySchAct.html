HOME
Learning Time Management and Prioritizing
One great benefit of being involved in school activities is that we will learn about time management and prioritizing things in life. We get very used to juggling a variety of different tasks and commitments thus we need to learn how to do these well, and getting involved in these activities can give us some practice at them.

NEWS
Making A Contribution
School activities allow us to make a contribution in some way. It shows that we are getting away from just thinking about ourselves and contributing to something else. This is important in our growth as a person.

ELECTIONS
Building Solid Relationship Skills
You will have the benefit of building solid relationship skills as well when get involved in school activities. We need to get involved in social activities and learn how to appropriately act in social situations.

PROFILE
Raises Self Esteem
Many times, being involved in school activities helps to raise the self esteem. There are many of us that feel worthless or that there is nothing we are good at. We struggle with self esteem, and these activities are a way that we can build self esteem. Everyone wants to find something that they are really good at, and school activities provide us with a way that we can get involved in something and really shine, giving our self esteem a boost.

EVENTS
Looks Great on College Applications
Yet another benefit of allowing ourselves to get involved in school activities is that it looks great on college resume. Most employers not only look at grades, but they take a look at activities that students are involved in while they are in college. They want to see that students are getting out there and doing more than just book work. The activities that we are involved in reveal a lot about ourselves, and definitely will say something to the employers.
